The CS OER

This project comprises two courses: Programming Fundamentals I, a.k.a., CS 1 (THECB: COSC 1436) and Programming Fundamentals II, a.k.a., CS 2 (THECB: COSC 1437). Additionally, we present modules on cybersecurity and data analytics to these two courses. 

The Servin Lab

The Servin's Lab is a platform designed and implemented by Dr. Christian Servin. The site serves as a repository for the open educational resources in computer science and secure scripting.

EPCC Peer-Led Team Learning

We are glad to have you here, in the first Computer Science peer-leading program at EPCC and Texas. Our mission is to be the bridge between students' classes and proficiency in programming.

How can I join the club?

To join, send us a message through our website, email, social media or talk to our officers in any of our meetings! 

Where are the meetings?

We usually have our meetings/workshops on the AST Building 3rd floor at EPCC Valle Verde (919 Hunter Dr, El Paso, TX 79915, US). But remember to always double check on any of our social media:)

Can I attend without a membership?

Yes, most events are open to non-members as well.

Are there coding workshops available?

We host coding workshops every month for all skill levels. Keep an eye on our social media!

What projects are available?

We offer a variety of projects ranging from Web Development, Cybersecurity, Games Development, etc.

How can I contribute to projects?

Reach out to project leads or attend our meetings!
